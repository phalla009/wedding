A Rainy Tuesday
The bookstore where it all began

Me & Crush · 2026

It was a rainy Tuesday in the city when Sarah and James first locked eyes across a crowded bookstore. He was reaching for the last copy of a novel she had been searching for all week — and somehow, that small collision of fate changed everything.

They spent the next three hours talking over coffee, forgetting the rain entirely. What started as a conversation about books became a conversation about life, dreams, and every little thing in between.

"What started as a conversation about books became a conversation about everything."

Three years later, on a quiet evening walk through the golden hills of Tuscany, James dropped to one knee — and Sarah said yes before he could finish asking.
